Adopt

SavePets’s mission is to provide forever homes for the hundreds of animals that need our help. But, as important as it is to ensure that the animals are a suitable match for you, we also want to ensure that you’re in the right position to adopt one. Our adoption process is simple: select which animal you’re interested in, fill out an application form and if selected, there will be a home visit to get to know you a bit more and for further instructions. Note that due to the high volume of adopters we get, you may not hear back from us unless you are selected to move forward with the home visit. However, this doesn’t mean that you don’t meet the criteria. We encourage you to stay updated on our “adopted” to check whether an adopter has already been found.
Thanks for adopting instead of buying!
